Palak Paneer (Recipe)

Palak Paneer is very famous Indian cuisine which is mainly served as main course with roti/chapati or naan. Palak Paneer is Indian cottage cheese made with spinach curry to describe in short.

Ingredients

  • Spinach 500 gms(1 bunch)
  • Water (as per need)
  • Oil 2 tbsp
  • Cumin 1 tsp
  • Green chilli 1 (chopped)
  • Garlic 1 tbsp (finely chopped)
  • Onions 2 tbsp (finely chopped)
  • Chilli powder 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
  • Cumin powder 1/2 tsp
  • Paneer cubes 500 gms
  • Cream (as per need)
  • Butter (as per need)
  • Kasuri Methi (optional)
    •

Instructions

So here's how we make it…

  • For making spinach puree

    1. Take 500gm spinach and wash it with water.
    2. Prepare boiling hot water in pan.
    3. Add spinach to boiling water and blanch it for two minutes.
    4. Rinse the blanced spinach with cold water and remove the excess water.
    5. Grind spinach in mixer till it makes a fine puree.

  • Making the curry

    1. Put a pan on medium heat with 2 tbsp oil in it.
    2. Add 1 tsp cumins, 1 chopped green chilli, 1 tbsp finely chopped garlic.
    3. Wait till garlic turns brown and then add 2 tbsp chopped onion in pan.
    4. Heat for 2 minutes and then add spinach puree we made earlier with salt according to need.
    5. Add 1 tsp chilli powder, 1/4 Turmeric powder and 1/2 tsp cumin powder (with optional Kasuri Methi) and mix it.
    6. Lastly, Add paneer cubes(200gm) and cook for 3 minutes.

  • Serve it hot with cream and butter on top.

Bonus Tips

  • Frying the paneer

    • You can fry the paneer cubes in some oil, dunk it into hot water for a few seconds, squeeze and set aside to be added to the gravy later. This makes the paneer softer.

  • Adding red chillis

    • You can add a couple of green chillies to the spinach while grinding instead of adding green chilli (while making) or using red chilli powder. I prefer using chopped green chilli

  • Making silky smooth curry

    • For a silky smooth flavour in your Palak Paneer, you can add the puree of cashews or almonds (1-2 tsp) while cooking.

  • Making it Vegan

    • Try substituting the paneer for tofu or try a homemade vegan mozzarella.
